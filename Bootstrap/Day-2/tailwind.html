TOURIST SPOTS

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal in Agra is a world-famous monument and a symbol of love. Built with white marble, it is known for its stunning architecture and rich history.

Alleppey

Alleppey is renowned for its serene backwaters, houseboat cruises, and picturesque landscapes. It offers a peaceful escape into Kerala's natural beauty.

Goa

Goa is India's most popular beach destination, known for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, water sports, and Portuguese heritage.

Leh-Ladakh

Leh-Ladakh is a paradise for adventure enthusiasts, featuring rugged mountains, crystal-clear lakes, ancient monasteries, and spectacular landscapes.

Mahabalipuram

Mahabalipuram is famous for its ancient rock-cut temples, shore monuments, and UNESCO-listed heritage sites that showcase the brilliance of Pallava architecture.

Marina

Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world, located in Chennai. It offers beautiful sunrise views, cool sea breezes, and a lively atmosphere with food stalls and local attractions.
