Software Quality Assurance: A Review of Literature"

This literature review provides a comprehensive overview of software quality assurance methodologies, metrics, and techniques. It synthesizes existing research from both industry and academia to offer insights into the various approaches used to ensure software quality.

"Testing Techniques and Strategies: A Comprehensive Review"

This survey paper offers a thorough examination of testing techniques and strategies employed in software testing. It covers a wide array of topics including black-box testing, white-box testing, regression testing, and automation testing, presenting a comprehensive landscape of the field.

"Challenges in Software Quality Assurance: A Systematic Literature Review"

This systematic literature review categorizes and analyzes the challenges faced in software quality assurance. It identifies various dimensions of challenges such as process-related, resource-related, and technological challenges, providing valuable insights for practitioners and researchers.
