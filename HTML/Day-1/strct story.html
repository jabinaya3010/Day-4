Intro

One of the most unforgettable moments in my life was the birth of my younger brother. He was born when I was fifteen years old, and it brought me immense happiness. Before that, we used to feel alone, but after he was born, it felt like we had someone who would always be there for us — a source of entertainment and a lifelong companion. He was such a tiny little baby. When they first placed him in my hands, I felt extremely happy and emotional. Even now, I continue to care for him with the same love and affection.I was the one who mostly raised my younger brother with a lot of love and care. My mother was there too, but I spent most of the time with him. I stayed beside him, took care of him, and did everything for him as he grew up. I practically raised him in my own hands. So now, living away from him feels a little difficult and emotional for me. Even today, I still see him as my baby. His name is Raghu — my dear little Raghu. Love you, Raghu.
