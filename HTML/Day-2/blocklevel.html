My Younger Brother

I was the one who mostly raised my younger brother with a lot of love and care. My mother was there too, but I spent most of the time with him.

I stayed beside him, took care of him, and did everything for him as he grew up. I practically raised him in my own hands.

So now, living away from him feels a little difficult and emotional for me. Even today, I still see him as my baby.

His name is Raghu — my dear little Raghu.

Love You, Raghu ❤️
