My Younger Brother

I was the one who mostly raised my younger brother with a lot of love and care. My mother was there too, but I spent most of the time with him.I stayed beside him, took care of him, and did everything for him as he grew up. I practically raised him in my own hands.So now, living away from him feels a little difficult and emotional for me. Even today, I still see him as my baby.When they first placed him in my hands, I felt extremely happy and emotional. Even now, I continue to care for him with the same love and affection. His name is Raghu — my dear little Raghu.

Love You, Raghu ❤️
